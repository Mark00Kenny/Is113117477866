Mark and Music

My interest in music.

I developed a keen interest in music from a young age. All of my brothers and sisters were musicians, and it was only natural that I found a love for listening to and playing music from being around them. At the age of 4 I began learning how to play the violin, under the tutelage of Constantin Zanidachie and ever since then playing music has been a major part of my life ever since.

The Quartet

During my time as a musician, I was extremely lucky to be the violist in the 'Accelerando String Quartet.' Playing together with my close friends , Kevin Jansson, Seamus Hickey and Zara Finn was immense fun and along the way we managed to play at many different venues competitions, and courses. Past performances included various corporate and charity events including the annual Simon Ball and events for the chartered accountants of Ireland, aswell as concerts in the National Concert hall and Castalia Hall. In our 5 year stint we were tutored by Adrian Petcu and we received masterclasses from members of the ICO, Vanbrugh Quartet, Danel Quartet and the Chiral Quartet.

In my free time away from music and education I had a passion for writing. In the past year I decided to combine my two hobbies together and start writing songs as a means of expression. So far I've written four complete songs, three of which are on Youtube.

James Blunt

My favourite musician

James Blunt has always been my favourite singer/songwriter. Whenever I listen to his music I always find that I can relate to the lyrics and the sombre melodies always play upon my mood. Without a doubt 1973 would be my favourite song of his as I have many memories associated with that song and James Blunt's work also greatly inspired me to write my own music.
