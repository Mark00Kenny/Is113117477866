Platform as a service(PaaS

Platform as a Service (PaaS) is a category of cloud computing services that provides a platform allowing customers to develop, run, and manage applications without the complexity of building and maintaining the infrastructure typically associated with developing and launching an app. Cloud9 is the company we use to distribute this service at an annual subscription fee of €200.

Communication as a service (CaaS)

Communications as a Service (CaaS) is an outsourced enterprise communications solution that can be leased from a single vendor. Such communications can include voice over IP, instant messaging (IM), collaboration and videoconference applications using fixed and mobile devices. Google Mail is the company we use to distribute this service at an annual subscription fee of €400.
